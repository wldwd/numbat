Numbat Geospatial

Practical mapping for regional communities

Numbat Geospatial helps councils, regional organisations and community groups turn location data into clear, useful maps.

We create practical tools to support planning, engagement and decision-making, including interactive web maps, asset mapping, and spatial analysis

How we can help

  • Web Mapping Interactive maps for tourism trails, places of interest, community facilities, and public engagement.
  • Asset & Infrastructure Mapping Getting your assets into GIS — paths, drainage, trees, signage — so they're visible, searchable and easier to manage.
  • Active Transport Analysis Cycling and walking network mapping, route analysis, and data to support funding bids and infrastructure planning.
  • Spatial Analysis & Reports Turning messy data into maps and insights that inform decisions, from from demographic analysis to environmental context.
